Reality check — what kabadiwalas bring reshapes your machinery priority

Your sourcing profile reshapes the machinery priority. Mixed kabadiwala lots mean you can't avoid appliance processing.

What kabadiwalas actually bring you
Your Day 1 reality
~35% CEEW — old TVs, fridges, ACs, washing machines, stabilizers
~25% LSEEW — fans, irons, mixers, geysers, small appliances
~20% ITEW — old phones, chargers, routers, keyboards, some laptops
~10% Mixed cables, wires, transformers
~5% EETW — drills, sewing machines
~5% Misc — tube lights, batteries (segregate immediately)
Problem: If you can only process ITEW, you're rejecting or stockpiling 60-70% of what walks through your door. Kabadiwalas won't sell you the 20% IT stuff separately — they sell the whole lot or nothing.
This means your machinery staging flips
Revised Stage 1: Full mechanical line including appliance processing
Rs 1.5 - 2.5 Cr
Core line: Dismantling tables + shredder + magnetic sep + eddy current + density sep + conveyors + baling + wire stripper + weighing + data destruction + APCD + ETP

Plus appliance line: Compressor cutting + refrigerant recovery + oil recovery. This is only Rs 4-14L extra but unlocks the 35% CEEW intake that kabadiwalas bring.

CRT cutting: CRT TVs are declining but still in kabadiwala lots. Basic hot-wire system Rs 8-15L. Alternatively, segregate CRTs and send to another recycler initially.

Generates: Fe + Cu + Al certificates across ITEW + CEEW + LSEEW + EETW. Sell PCBs to gold refiners.
Once cashflow established and corporate contracts secured
Revised Stage 2: Add furnace + precious metal recovery
Rs 45L - 1.25Cr extra
Trigger: Corporate contracts bring high-grade PCBs (servers, mobile boards). At that point, selling to refiners at Rs 250-600/kg makes no sense when you can extract gold yourself.

Unlocks: Gold certificates (12th recycler in India with this capability). Copper ingots instead of scrap. Massive margin improvement on PCB-rich waste.

This is the real profitability inflection point. But it needs reliable PCB volume to justify the capex — which comes from corporate/producer contracts, not kabadiwalas.
Optional niche expansion
Stage 3: Tube light/CFL + medical devices
Rs 10-25L extra
Only if volume justifies. Mercury lamp recycling is a compliance requirement on the Annexure 1 list — but if you're not actively sourcing fluorescent waste, a basic crusher with carbon filter satisfies the inspection.
