ITEW IT & Telecom Equipment — 27 items EPR: ₹34K → ₹1.12L/T ▸
💻 Laptops, desktops, servers, phones, tablets, printers, scanners, routers, modems, UPS, inverters, fax machines, GPS
Material Composition (per tonne)
Copper
10-20%
Iron
15-20%
Plastic
20-30%
Gold
250-300g
Why it matters
Highest EPR certificate value of any category. PCB-dense — laptops and phones have the richest PCBs. Corporate IT disposals = bulk supply. This is your money category.
Processing Flow
Dismantling table → Separate: batteries, screens, PCBs → Shredder → Magnetic sep → Eddy current sep → Sorted: Fe / Cu / Al / PCBs
Machines Needed
Dismantling table Shredder Magnetic separator Eddy current separator Density separator Conveyor Bailing press Degausser / Data wiper
⚠ Data destruction is mandatory for corporate e-waste. PCBs sold to refiners at ~₹350/kg until you add gold recovery (Stage 2).

Why it matters
Your volume play — fridges, ACs, washing machines flow in bulk from kabadiwala channel. Heaviest iron content = good Fe certificates. But triggers the most extra machines.
Processing Flow (varies by appliance)
Fridges/ACs:
Compressor cutter → Refrigerant recovery → Oil recovery → Shredder → Sep line
CRT TVs:
CRT cutting machine → Lead glass separated → Shredder → Sep line
Machines Needed
Core mechanical line Compressor cutter Refrigerant gas recovery Oil recovery CRT cutting machine Wire stripper
⚠ CEEW triggers 4 extra machines (₹15-50L additional). Ask the supplier for the appliance sub-line quote separately.

MDW Medical Devices — 10 items EPR: ₹20K → ₹50K/T ▸
🏥 Radiotherapy, cardiology, dialysis, ventilators, nuclear medicine, lab diagnostics, medical freezers
SKIP IN PHASE 1. Requires biohazard decontamination infrastructure. Radiation equipment needs special protocols. Not worth the compliance overhead at 330 TPA scale. Revisit when you're at Config 4 scale.
